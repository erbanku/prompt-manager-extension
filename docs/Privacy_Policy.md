# **Privacy Policy for Prompt Manager Extension**

Last updated: Thursday, November 06, 2025

---

**Prompt Manager** is a Chrome extension designed to help users organize and copy prompts efficiently. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

### 1. Data Collection

- **Local Storage Only:**
All prompts, preferences, and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage. We do **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data or prompt content on our own servers.

- **Backup & Restore (S3/WebDAV):**
If you choose to back up or restore your data with Amazon S3 or WebDAV, your prompts and settings are securely transferred **only** to the destination you configure. We do not access or retain any of this data.

- **No Analytics/Tracking:**
The extension does **not** use any analytics or tracking technologies.

---

### 2. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or disclose your data to any third party.
No prompt or usage information is ever transferred, except for backup/restore actions initiated by you.

---

### 3. Permissions

- **Storage:** Required to save prompts and preferences in your browser.
- **Host Permissions (S3/WebDAV):** Only used if you utilize backup or restore features, for user-initiated data transfers.
- **Side Panel:** Used for convenience, to access your prompt collection in Chrome’s side panel.

---

### 4. Security

Your data remains on your device unless you manually export it. When using remote backup, your data is transmitted securely to the provider you specify via their standard protocols.

---

### 5. User Control

You have full control over your prompts and settings. You may add, modify, delete, back up, or restore your prompts at any time.

---

### 6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy if required. Updates will be posted on our extension listing or support site.

---
